User Onboarding & First-Session Experience Audits

I work on the user journey of a product — discovery → signup → first session → early activation.

I break down:

  • what users see, understand, and feel
  • where friction shows up
  • what needs to be fixed or simplified
  • what experiments can improve activation

I’ve created a User Onboarding Audit Framework that covers: Discovery → Landing Narrative → Signup Flow → Personalization → First Task → Habit Formation → Analytics Setup.

What I deliver (3-4 business days)

  • Short walkthrough of signup + first-session flow with screenshots
  • 3–5 prioritized UX + copy fixes you can implement quickly
  • Suggested experiments and expected impact (benchmarks)
  • Optional analytics checklist (events, funnels, cohorts)
