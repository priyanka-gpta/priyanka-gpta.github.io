What do they offer?

Resource Library

  • GMAT concept guide with topic wise concise coverage, broken down into easy to understand bytes with practical examples.
  • Expert articles on tackling each GMAT section with action plans, time management techniques and journey maps for students seeking 2-4-6 months study plans.
  • Best and proven resources on practice materials arranged by difficulty level and concepts helping you build confidence gradually.
  • Get advice from experts on tackling weaker sections and mock test performance analysis

AI Study Companion

  • Create your custom personalized study plans based on your target score, available study time, and current performance level.
  • Smart problem solving with easy to follow step-by-step solutions that don't just explain WHAT to do, but HOW to think about each problem.
  • Track your progress and adapt while monitoring your performance to identify weak areas and adjust your study plan to strengthen them.

Community Support

  • Discord community connect with fellow GMAT aspirants, share strategies, and stay motivated through the journey.
  • Peer learning with live focus study sessions, discuss problems, share insights, and learn from experiences in a supportive environment.
  • Accountability Partner with study buddies and track progress to stay consistent and resilient.

To understand user behaviour with respect to their learning style and how they consume content or study.

Insights:

While users are comfortable with online medium, they still require someone to hold them accountable. They have a willingness to pay for online support and they have been exposed to online learning content before. They currently consume content from Instagram, Youtube, Reddit and X (Twitter).
