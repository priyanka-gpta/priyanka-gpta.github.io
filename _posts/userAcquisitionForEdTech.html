I would go for the below order for acquisition:

  1. Organic - The startup is Pre-PMF which requires a lot of feedback from initial users to get the product right and GMAT Examples is working towards creating founder brand on Reddit to introduce the AI Prep assistant to gain more users and build solid community through Discord.
  2. Referral Program - While the interest in physical coaching classes is going down, the tutors are now moving to online practice and they are looking for tech help to acquire new students. GMAT Examples plans to partner with Tutors who are shifting to online teaching to support their students with existing community and AI Assistant for 24/7 support and expert classes from the tutors.
  3. Content Loops: There is high quality content on GMAT concepts on blogs, the plan is to reuse the content to share on social media for better engagement.

Opportunities:

  1. Mobile First Solutions - The GMAT preparation market is seeing a significant shift towards mobile/online learning with students increasingly preferring to study on the go or access anywhere solutions. Unlike traditional coaching institutes, mobile first solutions offer convenience for busy professionals (ICP2) preparing for GMAT. Current market solutions fail to optimize for mobile experiences, leading to broken study sessions and poor user engagement. There's a growing demand for native mobile apps that offer personalised practice sessions, quick concept reviews, and progress tracking. With over 70% of students using smartphones as their primary study device, mobile first solutions represent a major in focus market opportunity.
  2. Custom Study Plans - The "one size fits all" approach to GMAT preparation is on a downwards trend. Students have diverse backgrounds, varied strengths, and different time commitments --> creating a strong demand for personalized study plans. The market lacks sophisticated solutions that can create truly personalized plans based on performance data, learning style, and study habits. This gap is being addressed with GMAT Examples AI driven assistant that can significantly improve study efficiency and score improvements.
  3. Support Group Dynamics - GMAT preparation can be an isolating experience, and students are actively seeking community support during their journey. While online forums exist, there's a clear opportunity for structured and moderated support groups. These groups can provide accountability, motivation, and shared learning experiences that are currently missing from most GMAT prep solutions.
  4. Performance Analytics - There are a lot of resources out there with practice materials but detailed performance analytics remain a largely underserved area in GMAT preparation. Students need more than just scores â€“ they want insights into their learning patterns, weak areas, and improvement plans.
  5. Custom Learning Paths - Current GMAT prep materials often follow rigid, linear progressions that don't adapt to student performance. There's a significant opportunity in creating custom learning paths that evolve based on student interactions and performance. These adaptive paths can ensure students spend more time on areas needing improvement and also quickly progressing through mastered concepts.
  6. High Appetite to Pay- The most promising opportunity lies in the willingness of serious GMAT takers to invest in quality preparation resources. With the GMAT being a crucial component of business school applications, students are willing to pay premium prices for solutions that improve their chances of success. The average serious GMAT candidate spends INR 75000 on preparation materials and services. This high payment appetite allows for the development of premium and feature rich solutions.

Detailing Content Loops

The content strategy begins with identifying and organizing our key content creators and distribution channels.

Content Creator: GMAT tutors, Top Scorers, Community participants

Distributor: Social Media Team

Channel: Reddit, Instagram, Discord

Our content creation team will consist of in house GMAT experts who bring deep subject knowledge, showcasing use of insights generated from our AI Prep Buddy that provide user-generated content from success stories and community discussions showing Social Proof that it helps students with their study practice.

Below are the interconnected content loops:

  1. Educational Content Loop: Designed to show our authority in GMAT preparation while driving organic traffic through SEO-optimized content. By creating GMAT guides, breaking down complex topics into easy to consume posts and sharing actual user success stories, we build trust with potential users while providing valuable and shareable content. The unique experience from AI Prep Buddy responses and usage, we also add an innovative angle to our educational content which sets us apart from traditional GMAT resources.
  2. Community Engagement Loop: This focuses on building user generated content and creating social proof through active community participation. We feature community success stories and hosting group study sessions along with AI Prep Buddy use, we create a vibrant and inviting setup where peer-to-peer learning takes place. This loop showcases authenticity and also build community loyalty which initiates a cycle of engagement and growth.

Partner Program: Tutors

GMAT Prep Buddy will partner with qualified experts/tutors (720+ GMAT score or teaching experience) who can refer students to our platform using unique tracking codes.

Tutors earn a base commission on student subscriptions and incremental opportunities through a tiered reward system based on the number of referred students:

  • Bronze: 1-5 students
  • Silver: 6-9 students
  • Gold: 10+ students

How do tutors track their metrics?

  1. There will be dedicated dashboard teaching resources, analytics, and real-time conversion tracking.
  2. We'll support them with a dedicated partner manager, and a WhatsApp support group.
  3. To maintain quality, we'll monitor student feedback and conduct quarterly performance reviews.
